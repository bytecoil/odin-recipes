Slow Cooker Hot Chocolate

Description

With just five ingredients, this delicious Crock-Pot hot chocolate is easy to make in the slow cooker.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups milk
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Combine milk, chocolate chips, cream, condensed milk, and vanilla in a slow cooker.
  2. Cover and cook on low, stirring occasionally, until chocolate chips are melted and texture is smooth, about 2 hours.
